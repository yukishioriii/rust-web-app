An additional store for fetching multiple product records at a time.

This trait is an implementation detail that lets us fetch more than one product. It will probably need to be refactored or just removed when we add a proper database. The fact that it's internal to domain::products though means the scope of breakage is a bit smaller. Commands and queries that depend on ProductStoreFilter won't need to break their public API.
